Spaghetti Carbonara

Prepared by: Rameshwor Rawal

Ingredients:

  • Spaghetti pasta
  • Eggs
  • Pancetta or bacon
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic (optional)

Instructions:

  1. Cook spaghetti according to package instructions.
  2. Fry pancetta or bacon until crispy.
  3. In a bowl, whisk eggs, Parmesan cheese, and black pepper.
  4. Once spaghetti is cooked, drain and immediately add to the pan with pancetta.
  5. Remove from heat and quickly mix in the egg mixture until the eggs thicken but do not scramble.
  6. Serve immediately, garnished with extra Parmesan and black pepper.

Spaghetti Carbonara has always been my favorite recipe because it's simple yet incredibly flavorful. The creamy sauce combined with the salty pancetta creates a perfect harmony of taste. It's a dish that never fails to comfort and satisfy.
